ABOUT THE GAME

THE GAME

The Last Pioneers is a PVE First Person Shooter. You and your squadron (other players), go around diffrent planets and moons in the solar system to complete a range of diffrent combat missions. Every mission is based off of one of the core gamemodes. Each player is 1 of the 3 classes. Your player class decides your starter stats, weapons, and playstyle.


PROGRAMS

I use Unreal Engine 5 and Blueprint programming to create the game. As well as Blender for 3D models and animations, Audacity for sound design, and GIMP for photoshopping different things.


STORY

It takes place in the year 2044, in an alternate timeline where the space race continues, and the USSR renames itself to the TCRS, or The Communist Republic of Sol. The space race leads to space war, and you and your squadron fight the soviets in ground and space battles around the solar system. As you progress through the game you uncover the hidden secrets that were being neglected all along..
