THE CONCEPT

The Last Pioneers is a singleplayer Tactical First Person Shooter set in the near future, where battles take place in the infinite abyss of the universe.


STORY

It takes place in the year 2035, only a few months after rouge Soviet AI took control of nuclear weapons, destroyed Earth, and killed 90% of the human race. Despite this, war continues among the 2 global powers. The remaining humans are forced to either live on space colonies, or join the military. You play as a starsoldier in the United States Space Force, and are given combat objectives that allow you to explore various parts of the solar system to fight for freedom.


ENVIRONMENTS

Every combat mission has its very own map, or landing zones around the solar system. As of right now, I plan to include Earth, The Moon, Mars, Europa, Titan, and maybe Venus as enviornments to house different maps. Each one of these planets/moons will have 2-3 combat objectives on it, some of them will be on the ground, a few will be in orbit around that planet/moon.


KEY FEATURES

  1. A narrative driven singleplayer campaign
  2. Survival mechanics that you must monitor
  3. Weapon modifications
  4. Different player classes and playstyles to mix up gameplay

TOOLS

I use Unreal Engine 5.5 and Blueprint programming to create the game (No C++ for now). I use Blender for 3D models and animations, Audacity for sound design, GIMP for graphic design, and Davinci Resolve 19 for video editing. Some assets are made completely on my own, others are either free or paid assets from various locations.
